# Marketing Data Analysis & AI Prediction Project

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **marketing and e-commerce data** to understand campaign performance, customer behavior, and sales outcomes. The goal is to transform raw data into **actionable insights** that support better marketing decisions and improve conversion rates.

The project combines **data analysis, visualization, and machine learning** to explore patterns in customer interactions and predict the behavior of new customers.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze **marketing campaign performance** and engagement metrics.
- Track the **customer journey** from website visit to final purchase.
- Identify **drop-off points** in the sales funnel affecting conversion rates.
- Evaluate **sales performance and product demand**.
- Analyze **customer reviews and customer sentiment**.
- Apply **AI and machine learning models** to predict customer types and behavior.

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Overview & Customer Journey
- Displays key marketing KPIs such as engagement rate, click-through rate (CTR), and conversion rate.
- Visualizes the **customer journey funnel** from homepage visit to checkout and purchase.
- Identifies major **drop-off points** where customers leave before completing a purchase.

### 2. Campaign Performance Analysis
- Analyzes marketing campaigns by impressions, engagement, likes, and views.
- Compares performance across different **content types** such as email marketing, social media, blogs, and video campaigns.

### 3. Sales & Product Performance
- Tracks key sales metrics including:
  - Total sales
  - Total revenue
  - Average Order Value (AOV)
- Identifies **top-performing products** and sales trends over time.

### 4. Customer Reviews & Sentiment Analysis
- Analyzes customer reviews to identify **common complaints and satisfaction drivers**.
- Highlights key issues affecting customer experience such as pricing, product quality, or delivery.

### 5. Geographic Analysis
- Visualizes customer and sales distribution across different countries and markets.
- Helps identify **high-performing regions** and areas that need improvement.

---

## AI & Predictive Modeling
In addition to descriptive analytics, this project includes **predictive modeling** to enhance business insights.

- Built a **machine learning model** to predict the **type of new customers** based on behavioral and transactional data.
- Applied **customer segmentation techniques** to classify customers into segments such as:
  - Loyal Customers
  - Champions
  - At Risk Customers
  - Customers Needing Attention
- These predictions help businesses **improve targeting strategies and personalize marketing campaigns**.

## Key Insights
The analysis revealed that although marketing campaigns generate **high engagement and website traffic**, the **conversion rate is relatively low due to significant drop-offs during the checkout stage**.

Customer sentiment analysis also showed that **pricing and perceived value** are among the most common reasons for customer complaints, which directly impacts purchase decisions.

Predictive modeling further helps identify **potential customer segments**, enabling more effective marketing strategies and improved customer targeting.

---

## Project Goal
The main goal of this project is to demonstrate how **data analytics and AI can be used together to transform marketing data into strategic business insights**, helping organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions.
